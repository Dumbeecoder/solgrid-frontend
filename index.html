How it works
Three steps to earn
1
Connect wallet and hold SGRID
Connect your Phantom or any Solana wallet. Hold 1,000,000 SGRID tokens to activate earning. No staking lockup required.
2
Run the prover
Download the open-source prover binary from GitHub. Point it at any synced Solana RPC — public endpoints work fine. No local node needed.
export NODE_RPC="https://api.mainnet-beta.solana.com"
./prover
3
Pass challenges and claim SOL
Every 5 minutes the prover answers a random chain challenge. Points accrue weekly and convert to SOL you claim on-chain via Merkle proof.
